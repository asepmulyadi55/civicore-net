Monthly Security Update: Enhanced Gate Protocols

Implementing new biometric access points and streamlining visitor registration to ensure a safer, more tranquil environment for all residents.

Dear Residents,

As part of our ongoing commitment to maintaining Dwipapuri Residence as a premier, secure, and serene community, we are rolling out significant upgrades to our security infrastructure this month. These enhancements are designed to seamlessly integrate with your daily life while providing robust protection.

New Biometric Access System

Starting November 1st, the main entrance and all pedestrian gates will transition to a state-of-the-art biometric access system. This new system utilizes advanced facial recognition and fingerprint scanning, replacing the old keycard system.

  • Faster Entry: No more fumbling for cards; access is granted in milliseconds.
  • Enhanced Security: Biometric data is encrypted and cannot be duplicated or shared.
  • Convenience: Ideal for returning from morning jogs or when carrying groceries.

Streamlined Visitor Registration

We are introducing a new mobile application module for visitor management. Residents will now be able to pre-register guests, delivery personnel, and service contractors directly from their smartphones.

Upon pre-registration, your guest will receive a secure, temporary QR code via SMS or email, allowing them swift entry without long wait times at the security pavilion. This minimizes congestion and ensures we have a clear, digital log of all non-residents entering the premises.

info Action Required

Please visit the Management Office before October 25th to register your biometric data. Registration takes less than 5 minutes per family member.

We appreciate your cooperation during this transition period. These upgrades are a crucial step in future-proofing our community and safeguarding the tranquility we all cherish at Dwipapuri Residence.
