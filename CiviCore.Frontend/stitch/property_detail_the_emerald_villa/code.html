About This Property

The Emerald Villa represents the pinnacle of contemporary residential design within the Dwipapuri Estate. Masterfully crafted to blend minimalist aesthetics with functional luxury, this two-story residence offers an unparalleled living experience bathed in natural light.

Upon entry, you are greeted by a soaring double-height foyer that seamlessly transitions into an expansive open-concept living and dining area. Floor-to-ceiling glass panels retract fully, dissolving the boundary between the pristine interior and the lush, meticulously landscaped private garden and lap pool outside.

The chef-inspired kitchen features bespoke European cabinetry, monolithic marble surfaces, and fully integrated premium appliances. Upstairs, the master sanctuary spans the entire rear wing, boasting a spa-like en-suite, generous walk-in dressing room, and a private terrace overlooking the estate's verdant canopy.
